About me

Welcome to my website ! I'm Jules GOMEL, a Phd Candidate (First Year PhD in the French System!), focused on Neurosciences and AI.

I'm currently working at ISAE-Supaero Neuroergonomics and Human Factor lab under the supervision of Dr. Frédéric Dehais.!

My work is focused on studying neuromarkers of the integration of visual information, for an intended use in BCI applications, following the work of the lab on cVEP and SSVEP.

Let's keep in touch if you want to know more ! I am very active on my LinkedIn, where I talk about research and more ! You can also reach me for any projects we could have together, related to work or hobbies.
