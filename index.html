About me

Welcome to my website ! I'm Jules GOMEL, a final year French master student in ISAE-Supaero MSc, focused on Neurosciences and AI.

I'm currently doing my end-of-study internship as a Research Engineer Intern in Neuroengineering at ISAE-Supaero Neuroergonomics and Human Factor lab under the supervision of Dr. Frédéric Dehais.

I will be starting a PhD focused on BCI in late 2024 so let's keep contact if you want to know more ! I am very active on LinkedIn, where I talk about research !

Studying Decision Making

Recently, I studied Daniel Kahneman's work on decision making, especially loss aversion, sunk cost and how it impacts our decisions and behaviour.

I find it really insightful regarding how it impacts the way we choose which game to play, which series to continue, which stuff to buy... and more!

Learn more with my LinkedIn posts on the topic, and this one too!
