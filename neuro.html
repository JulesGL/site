My work in Neuroengineering

My research interests

I'm dedicated to research in Neuroengineering because I really enjoy applying my engineering skills to solve problems for patients.

I'm really interested in Brain-Computer Interfaces for rehabilitation, neurofeedback and diagnosis.

If you're interested in these topics, let's keep in touch !

My technical skills

I have strong coding in Python skills, after various projects for classes and internship. I also worked a lot with Matlab for Signal Processing. Feel free to check my GitHub profile.

I also have a good knowledge of Machine Learning and Deep Learning, and I'm currently learning about BCI and EEG signal processing.

Finally, I'm also learning about UX/UI design as I've developed several user applications during projects and internships and web development to be able to enhance my website.

You can learn more about my projects and skills by looking at my CV !
